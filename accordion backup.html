▼ Yggdrasil Path

Wedge into the corner of the bushes and the building, then aim at the left corner of the small "slit" gap in Yggdrasil. Set mines.

Defender respawn path to point

Find the circular gold platform on left side height out of spawn. Stand on the nodule closer to the main spawn door. Aim up at Yggdrasil, and find the lowest small branch. Follow up to the small fork in the branch, then move your cursor up until you are slightly above the next branch up. Set mines.

Outside defender spawn #1

▼ Hall of Djalia

Turn left in spawn room, and jump onto the platform. Wedge yourself between the pillar and the wall, look up and into the gap where you can see the sky. Aim to the top right of the leafy plant and set mines.

Point A outside of cart door

Wedge yourself into the corner to the right outside of spawn 2. Aim up following the right side of the Halo ring until your cursor intersects with the hanging plants. Set mines.

Bottom staircase in second point choke

▼ Spider Islands

Web out of spawn to behind the sign. Line up in the center of the sign, then walk backwards until you collide with the wall behind you. Aim up and use the top of the "/" in your health numbers to line up with the tip of the golden triangle created by the connecting strand between the two web strands. Set mines. Note: There are lots of variations of this, feel free to mess around.

Defender holding corner on first point

Climb the wall towards the sign, and wedge yourself in the left corner with the spider web. Aim up at the cracks in the sky and find the dark shard at the 2:00 position. Follow the bottom edge up until you meet the tip of a triangle below your line. Line up with that tip, then go slightly to the right. Set mines.

Food truck outside defender spawn #1

▼ Symbiotic Surface

Get behind the big tube outside of the choke point. Aim up at the big star in the sky, then follow down and to the left, between the two medium stars. Any area around there will work. Set mines.

On point #1

Square up with the middle of the box to the right of the path. Aim up and follow the two points of the structure up and aim at the bright medium star. Drop your cursor below the star and set mines.

Box near point #1

▼ Midtown

Center yourself in front of the broken police car to the right of the main gate. Aim up at the "notch" in the building roof, then up a little farther so that you're just shy of even with the outcropping to the right. Sort of where the corner points you. Set mines.

Second corner on point #2

Set yourself next to the ambulance, clipping into the side-mirror. Aim up at Avengers tower, where the helipad meets the rest of the building. Follow that line up until your cursor is equidistant from both the top and first of the corners of the top triangle.

Defender walkout on point #3
