Nest Locations

Basic nest spots for each Convoy/Convergence map

Below are two basic nest spots for each map. Click on the heading to view the content!

▼ Yggdrasil Path

Setting up in this crevice holds space on the cart path, and gives your team a place to stand on defense.

This spot on the corner is difficult to push into without walking into the mines, and is difficult to notice in the heat of battle.

▼ Shin Shibuya

The main choke can be hard to push even without Peni's nest, but setting up here can delay a push even further. Once this setup fails, you can use the other bits of cover nearby to re-establish your setup.

Holding the arcade behind the point can be great on both offense and defense. You also have access to a health pack in this room!

▼ Spider Islands

This spot can be very tricky for the attackers to uproot you from. With only a few doors in, you can easily mine the entry points and even contest the cart from the doorway.

Holding at the base of these stairs grants control of the staircase and denies the flank to the right. Enemies will have to walk into the webs to remove this nest.

▼ Hall of Djalia

Control the left flank with this nest, which can cover into the health pack room. However, it is vulnerable to pushes from the ramp side.

When coming back to re-contest the point, the garage door can provide good cover and a safe space for your team to push from.

▼ Symbiotic Surface

Holding this room can deny both major access corridors to the point. By mining the main pathway and defending your right flank, enemies can struggle to remove a Peni who is set up in this corner.

This spot is great for when you need to deny access to the point itself. Supports and reinforcing DPS often will try to join their tanks on point, and meet their end to your spider drones.

▼ Midtown

This laundromat gives great sightlines over the attacker spawn, and can be extended down to the cart path with your webs. Mining up the entrance to the window will often catch enemies off guard as they try to remove you.

If you need to hold closer to your team but still want to hold forward, setting up in the diner is quite powerful. This spot has great cover, and spreads webs easily to the cart path.
